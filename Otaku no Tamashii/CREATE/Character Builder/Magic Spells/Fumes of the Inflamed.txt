"O Fire, kindling wonder, the dancing luster, for why have you taken so? It was I that fueled you, it was I that sustained you, must you engulf even the hand that fed you? The flare of your passion has turned dark, a mere remnant of your past radiance. Take my hand and heed my call. Once more we rise; from the ashes, we emerge. Your rebirth becomes my unbending will. Let this be the time of redemption. Fumes of the Inflamed!"

An AOE magic that turns every smoke trail in the vicinity along with its flames into
elusive flame arrows that can turn to smoke to dodge attacks.
