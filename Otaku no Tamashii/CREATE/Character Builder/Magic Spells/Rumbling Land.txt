O Earth, speak once more and deliver your will. Heaven or hell; pass upon your judgment on the mortals before you. Rise: Rumbling Land!  ----->  upward/rise

O Earth, speak once more and deliver your will. Heaven or hell; pass upon your judgment on the mortals before you. Fall: Rumbling Land!  ----->  downward/cave-in

A magic that can either throw the land upward or sink downward.
