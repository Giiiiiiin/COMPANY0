By having mana around you, it creates the phenomenon called Element Ingression. It is what allows the person who released 
the mana to be able to use the power of the 11 elements. In addition, the caster of the magic cannot be hurt by the magic 
created from their own mana.

While the cast phase is the part at the end of a chant. It mentions the magic spell's name and makes mana undergo element 
ingression, creating magic.
