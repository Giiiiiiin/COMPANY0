Name comes from the Zeldian word Glassmetal

An incredibly fragile metal that can only be mended by master blacksmiths due to its incredible fragility.
The metal itself is not suited as a tool.
However, the metal possesses the highest compatibility with spirits, giving it the name of Iordr meaning, Spirit Metal

This is the metal Kuro Yaiba is made out of.
